Chicken Thighs

Description

Sesame chicken thighs are a flavorful and satisfying dish that balances sweet, savory, and slightly spicy elements in every bite. The marinade—made with sesame oil, soy sauce, honey, sriracha, and rice vinegar—infuses the chicken with a rich, umami depth while adding just a touch of heat and tang. As the chicken cooks, the skin becomes crisp and golden, while the inside stays tender and juicy, creating a contrast in both texture and flavor that makes the dish especially appealing.

Cooking the chicken in an air fryer makes the process quick and efficient, delivering a crispy finish without the need for excess oil. Once rested, the thighs are topped with fresh green onion and toasted sesame seeds, which add brightness and a subtle crunch. This dish pairs easily with rice, noodles, or vegetables, making it a versatile option for both weeknight dinners and more composed meals.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 tablespoon sriracha sauce
  • 1 teaspoon rice vinegar
  • 2 pounds chicken thighs
  • 1 green onion, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons toasted sesame seeds

Steps

  1. Combine sesame oil, soy sauce, honey, sriracha, and vinegar in a large bowl. Add chicken and stir to combine. Cover and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Preheat an air fryer to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Drain marinade from the chicken.
  3. Place chicken thighs skin-side up in the basket of the air fryer. Cook for 5 minutes. Flip and cook an additional 10 minutes.
  4. Transfer chicken to a plate and let rest 5 minutes before serving. Garnish with green onion and sesame seeds.
